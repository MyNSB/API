Platforms

MyNSB is avaliable on almost all major platforms. MyNSB can either be downloaded through the appstore or through a beta channel.

If you wish to gain access to the Beta channel please contact NSBHS Visions directly.

iOS

The myNSB application is avaliable on Apple App store for students with an iOS device. Do note however that the minimum SDK requirement for the iOS app is 10.
Core Dev: Hanyuan Li

Android

myNSB is avaliable on the Google Play store for easy download for students with an Android device. Do note however that the minimum OS requirement is Android Lollipop.
Core Dev: Varun Sethu

Web

The myNSB app is avaliable as a Web application for students without smart devices, do note that this platform is not avaliable during the Beta Release.
Core Dev: Gabriel Phua
